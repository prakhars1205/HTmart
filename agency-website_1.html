FashionX — 400% ROAS
in 60 Days

Category: Social Media · Performance Duration: 60 Days Platform: Meta · Instagram Year: 2024
👗
▶
Campaign Video Placeholder · 2:34

The Challenge

FashionX was a D2C fashion brand with great products and zero digital presence. They'd tried running ads themselves with minimal results and came to us needing a complete social media and performance strategy from scratch.

Our Approach

We started with a deep brand audit and competitive analysis, then built a content strategy centred on short-form video — Reels that showcased their products in real-life scenarios rather than sterile product shots. Simultaneously, we rebuilt their Meta Ads funnel from the ground up with proper audience segmentation and creative testing.

Our content team produced 3 Reels per week — each with multiple hooks tested against each other. The winning creative concepts were then amplified through paid spend. We also ran a micro-influencer campaign with 12 creators in the fashion niche for authentic social proof.

The Results

Within 60 days, FashionX went from struggling with 2x ROAS to a consistent 400% ROAS. Their Instagram grew from 2,000 to 28,000 followers organically. Monthly revenue crossed ₹1Cr for the first time.

SpiceLane — 2M Impressions
in 2 Weeks

Category: Influencer · ContentDuration: 2 WeeksPlatform: Instagram · YouTubeYear: 2024
🍕
▶
Campaign Highlights · 0:59

The Challenge

SpiceLane was launching a new range of regional Indian spice blends and needed massive awareness fast. With a limited budget, they needed smart influencer selection and viral-worthy content.

Our Approach

We identified 40 micro and mid-tier food creators across Instagram and YouTube whose audiences matched SpiceLane's target demographics. We briefed each creator with cooking challenges using the spice blends — organic, authentic content that didn't feel like an ad. We also produced a hero video for paid amplification.

The Results

The campaign generated over 2M organic impressions in just 14 days. Three videos went viral independently. SpiceLane sold out their initial stock within the first week of launch.
